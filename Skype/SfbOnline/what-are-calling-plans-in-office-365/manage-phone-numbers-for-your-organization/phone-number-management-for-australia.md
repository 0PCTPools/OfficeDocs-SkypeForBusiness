# Phone number management for Australia
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Australia is only available to preview and early adopters customers. For more information, see [Skype Preview Programs](https://www.skypepreview.com). 

In Australia we have partnered with Telstra for the purchase and provisioning of PSTN Calling Plans, Telstra sell the PSTN calling plan licenses and provide the support for the service, whilst the Audio conferencing Service is still sold and supported by Microsoft .

For more information on the [Telstra Service](http://aka.ms/TelstraVoicePlan)

Use this table to find information on getting and managing phone numbers in Australia for Skype for Business Online and Microsoft Teams. 

For more information, see [Manage phone numbers for your organization](manage-phone-numbers-for-your-organization.md).
  
|**What you want to do?**|**Do these steps and download the correct forms**|
|:-----|:-----|
|Get new user phone numbers for Calling Plan in Australia.   <br/> | Please contact Telstra at the above link for new stock, Telstra will add the new stock into your tenant for you. <br/> 
|Get new service phone numbers for use with AutoAttendant & CallQueues for Australia.   <br/> |Please contact Telstra at the above link for new stock, Telstra will add the new stock into your tenant for you.  <br/>|
|Get new service phone numbers for Audio Conferencing for Australia.   <br/> |[Please contact us](mailto:ptnapac@microsoft.com) |
|You need to get phone numbers transferred to O365 for use with users or AutoAttendant & CallQueues  <br/> |Please contact Telstra at the above link for Port in of these numbers, Telstra will add the new stock into your tenant for you. <br/> |
|You need to get phone numbers transferred (Ported) to O365 for use with Audio Conferencing  |[Please contact us](mailto:ptnapac@microsoft.com) |
|Changes to the Emergency address assigned to a given Calling Plan users phone number. |Please contact Telstra at the above link for Emergency address changes, they can update the information for you.|
|You need to change a user number to a service number. |This service is not supported in Australia.
