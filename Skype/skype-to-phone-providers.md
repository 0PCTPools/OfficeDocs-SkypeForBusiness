# Skype-to-phone providers

 Skype-to-phonesyndicated partners approved by Microsoft are available for organizations that can provide voice mail and auto attendant features for Skype for Business Online organizations.
  
    
    


## Want to know more?

Using Skype-to-phone, users can make calls to and receive calls but requires that you set up an account with either a mobile phone carrier or a public switched telephone network (PSTN) carrier, depending on your Skype for Business Online service plan.
