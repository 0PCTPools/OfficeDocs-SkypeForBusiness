View guest users in a team
==========================
Admins and team owners can see all members of a team, including guests, via **Manage team** in Microsoft Teams.  [See who's on a team or in a channel ](https://support.office.com/article/see-who-s-on-a-team-or-in-a-channel-5c6be9be-9c45-4a0f-a1a0-f332b23cb6b7) has more details.
